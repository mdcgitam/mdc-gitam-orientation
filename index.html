01 About MDC

Meta Developer Communities was inaugurated on 4th January 2023 as Meta Developer Circles, in official partnership with Meta. When Meta discontinued its global Developer Circles program in 2024, we didn't dissolve — we evolved.

Rebranded and evolved as an independent, student-driven network, MDC continues the same founding mission: technical collaboration, peer-to-peer learning, and mentorship from people who push you further than you'd push yourself. Today MDC operates in partnership with the GITAM Career Guidance Centre and Student Life, giving every project, hackathon, and initiative real institutional weight.

07

Rules and
Regulations.

A community runs on trust. Here's what's expected of every member — kept simple, not buried in fine print.

01

Conduct

Represent MDC with dignity — no negativity about the club in front of students, faculty, or dignitaries. Confidentiality on internal plans and strategy is non-negotiable. Respect every member; no false information, no backbiting.

02

Communication

Respond to club communications within the stipulated time. Group chats are a no-message zone from 11:00 PM to 5:00 AM, except genuine urgency.

03

Leave Policy

Notify your Domain Lead at least 48 hours in advance if you can't attend. In genuine emergencies, 4 hours' notice is acceptable. Approval sits with the Executive Body.

04

Termination Grounds

Consistently unfulfilled responsibilities, 3 consecutive unexcused absences, or violation of MDC's code of conduct can result in removal — decided at the Executive Body's discretion.

08

Certificate
Eligibility.

Certificates are earned, not automatic. Even if minimum criteria are met, MDC reserves the right to withhold a certificate if contribution falls below expected standards.

01

Attendance Requirement

Maintain 75% overall attendance across Events, Domain Meetings, and Core Team Meetings — with 50%+ encouraged in each category individually.

02

Domain Requirement

Deliver on your domain's core tasks — events organized, projects shipped, and reviews cleared on schedule — as laid out for your domain above.

03

Statement of Contribution

Submit your SOC on MDC's standard template by 03rd April 2027, documenting your tasks, initiatives, and impact — the official record considered for certification.

Letter of
Selection.

The Letter of Selection is MDC's formal, signed confirmation that you're officially part of the community.

  • What it is — an official letter issued by MDC confirming your selection and role within the community.
  • Who receives it — every newly selected member, right at the start of their MDC journey.
  • How it works — the club issues the letter, and the member signs to accept and acknowledge it.
  • Why it matters — it stands as an official, signed record of your selection into MDC.
